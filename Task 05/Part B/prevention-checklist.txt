Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making transactions
- Do not accept unknown payment requests
- Use strong passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions

UPI Safety Tips:
- Remember that entering UPI PIN means you are paying money, not receiving
- Always check the name of the receiver before approving any request

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
